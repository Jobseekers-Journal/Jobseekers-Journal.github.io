Job Profile:

The Data Analyst Intern will support the implementation of the enterprise-wide data management operating model and contribute to the advancement of data management capabilities across the organization. This role will work closely with Business Technology teams to achieve these objectives.

Responsibilities include:

  • Assist in the implementation of a federated data governance strategy by supporting communication and collaboration among stakeholders.
  • Collaborate with data management stakeholders to support the implementation of data management capabilities in accordance with established guidelines and policies.
  • Contribute to the identification of critical data assets and elements, working under the guidance of senior analysts to support data remediation efforts.
  • Support efforts to connect business data stewards and technical data stewards with the necessary tools and resources, facilitating trainings.
  • Participate in identifying critical data assets, working with federated data management teams to gather information and analyze data prioritization needs.
  • Develop procedures and best practices for the implementation of data management capabilities to enable self-service capabilities of federated teams.
  • Innovate on data governance best practices by exploring ways to automate and drive efficiency through AI capabilities.
  • Build productive partnerships with various technology, legal, security, and analytics/data management teams across Salesforce.

Required Skills:

  • Currently working towards an undergraduate degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or other relevant majors (must be returning to school after the internship).
  • Basic understanding of data management concepts such as data quality, metadata management, and data governance.
  • Experience gaining alignment across a diverse range of stakeholders.
  • Enthusiasm for data and a desire to learn and grow in the field of data governance.
  • Effective communication skills to drive consensus and clearly articulate governance strategy, vision, and business values.
  • Familiarity with data maturity models and Salesforce products is a plus.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with data analysis tools and techniques.
  • Excellent business and technical communication skills at all levels of the organization.
  • Actively contributes to team discussions, demonstrates initiative in learning new tools and methodologies, and supports team in data-related tasks.
  • Ability to be agile, proactive, and comfortable with ambiguous specifications.
  • Effectively communicates data insights through clear reports and presentations.
